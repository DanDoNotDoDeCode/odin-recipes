Burger Recipe

Description

This recipe will include the material(s) and step-by-step instructions on how to make a delicious Bacon Cheese-Burger. If followed correctly, it guarantees a delicious and juicy Bacon Cheese Burger for the whole family.

Ingredients And Directions

  • 1 1/2 lbs of ground beef
  • Salt and Black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder

For Toppings

  • 8 sliced of bacon
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 burger buns
  • lettuce leaves
  • tomato slices
  • pickles
  • sliced red onions
  • any condiment(s)
  1. Prepare the bacon
    • Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy
    • Drain on paper towels and set aside.
  2. Form the burger patties
    • Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions
    • Gently shape into patties slightly larger than the buns
    • Season both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Cook the burgers
    • Heat a skillet, grill, or griddle over medium-high heat.
    • Cook the patties for 3/4 minutes per side for medium doneness.
    • During the last minute of cooking, place a cheese slice on each patty to melt.
  4. Toast the buns (optional but recommended)
    • lightly butter the buns and toast them on a skillet/grill until golden brown
  5. Assemble the burgers
    • assemble all the Ingredients to your liking

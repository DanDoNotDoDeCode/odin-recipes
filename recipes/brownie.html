Brownie recipe

Description

This recipe will give you one of the best brownie recipes made by lord odin, Have fun and enjoy!

Ingredients and Directions

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
    • Grease or line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter
    • In a medium saucepan or microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter.
    • Stir in sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla while the butter is still warm.
  3. Add eggs
    • Beat in eggs, one at a time, until the batter is smooth and glossy.
  4. Mix dry ingredients
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, salt, and baking powder.
    • Gently stir into the wet mixture until just combined don't over mix.
  5. Bake
    • Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
    • Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter).
  6. Cool and cut
